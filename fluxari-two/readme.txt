== Description ==

Fluxari is a dark, sleek, and versatile block-based theme, designed for agencies, businesses, and creatives who seek a bold and professional online presence. With full site editing (FSE) capabilities, it provides complete design control, allowing you to customize every detail effortlessly. Its modern dark aesthetic enhances portfolios, corporate websites, and marketing projects, creating a striking visual impact. Elevate your brand with a theme that seamlessly blends style, performance, and flexibility.
